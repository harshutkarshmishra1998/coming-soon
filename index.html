About SCEECS

conference
SCEECS produces bright young minds that keep the scintillating flame glimmering by instilling the importance of research. SCEECS, a biennial event that lures young trailblazers from across the nation and provides them with an opportunity and a platform to showcase their brilliance, through a merge of technical workshops, conferences, and guest lectures by prominent personalities. The main directive of the event is the research paper conference, with a prime objective is to facilitate research work and promote researchers for the development of our country. It felicitates accessibility and availability of the original work, publishing it and uploading it on the world wide web, making it more portable to anyone in the world.

Workshop on Data Structures and Algorithms by Geeks for geeks

Gear up for a labyrinthine journey through the realm of Data Structure & Algorithms! The IEEE-MSB team is thrilled to unravel a two-day technical workshop on the 18th and 19th of February 2023, where you'll delve into the enigmatic beauty of fractals of DSA and how they can be applied to your career trajectory.

Unlock the fourth dimension with two days of the power-packed workshop followed by an interactive session with the oratorical prowess of none other than, the distinguished among engineers, Mr. Sandeep Jain, Founder of GeeksforGeeks, as he takes us through this labyrinth of knowledge. Don't let placements befuddle you any longer, come and discover how enigmatic fractals can be a panacea for your career quandaries!
