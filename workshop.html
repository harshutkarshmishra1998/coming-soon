      ABOUT WORKSHOP

      The GEEKS FOR GEEKS workshop on Data Structures and Algorithms is an event designed to provide attendees with a comprehensive understanding of the fundamental concepts and techniques of data structures and algorithms. The workshop will cover topics such as sorting and searching algorithms, data structures like arrays, linked lists, trees, and graphs, and advanced algorithms like dynamic programming and greedy algorithms. It will also provide hands-on experience through coding exercises and implementation of these concepts.

      This workshop will provide you with a solid foundation in the fundamental concepts and techniques of computer science, which are essential for a wide range of applications and fields.

      • Data structures and algorithms form the backbone of computer science and are used in many areas such as software development, computer systems, databases, artificial intelligence, and more.
      • Attendees also have the opportunity to learn from experts in the field and gain insights and perspectives that are difficult to obtain from books or online resources.
      • Additionally, it provides a platform for attendees to network with like-minded individuals and establish professional connections.
